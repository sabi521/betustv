The Premier League Show Picks & Analysis

The Premier League Show is live every Thursday at 10:00 AM ET! The show delivers the best analysis, betting insights, and comprehensive information on every EPL matchday.

Our host, Gordon 'Flash' Watson, is joined by Mina Rzouki and Brad Thomas to provide you with winning bets and plenty of intel to help you make the most of the Premier League. Don't miss it—join us!

Host & Guests

A former Premier League striker turned lead analyst, Gordon 'Flash' Watson brings two decades of top-flight experience to every matchday breakdown. He fronts the show with the sharp reads and honest calls that made him a fan favourite.

A seasoned betting analyst, Brad Thomas has built his reputation on fearless value picks and data-driven insight across Europe's biggest leagues. Expect straight talk, standout numbers, and the odd contrarian shout.

Award-winning football journalist Mina Rzouki covers the tactical side of the beautiful game, connecting the numbers to what actually happens on the pitch. She adds the context that turns a hunch into a confident bet.
